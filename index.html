problems that faces humans

  • The problem of food insecurity

    The problem of human food insecurity in relation to the ocean relates to the difficulty of obtaining sufficient amounts of healthy and nutritious food from the oceans. The effects of environmental pollution, overfishing and climate changes may lead to a shortage of food resources in the oceans. It is important to develop sustainable fishing practices and protect the marine environment to maintain food security for humans and marine life.

  • The problem of lack of energy

    Ocean powerlessness can be due to several reasons, such as underexploitation of renewable energy sources in the oceans such as tropical energy and ocean tides. Also, technical and economic challenges in extracting and converting energy from the oceans play a role. There are also environmental issues such as ocean pollution and climate change that affect the possibility of effectively exploiting marine energy.
